Q. I can't login, what can I do?

A. Sometimes the root of the problem can be from having a wrong server IP in the settings. Try changing your IP in settings or asking someone to inform you of any server changes.

Q. How can I scout a specific team?

A. You can scout a team by going to the all teams page and clicking on a team, or you can go to the all matches page and click on a match that said team is participating in, and then select the team number from the table.

Q. What does the highlighted row in the all matches section mean?

A. The highlighted row is, in fact the match that is currently taking place. It is based on time, so it may not be accurate if there were to be delays.

Q. What are some server IP's I can try?

A. Maybe if you are running the server locally you can try "localhost" or "127.0.0.1". Otherwise, another common modem IP is "192.168.0.X" Note: X is a number. e.g "2" or "12".
